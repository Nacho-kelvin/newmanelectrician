OUR STORY

Built on the tools, not just the trade

Newman Electrical Services started as a one-man callout service in 2019 and has grown into a licensed contracting team, without losing the habit of showing up on time and doing the job right the first time.

HOW WE STARTED

From single callouts to full contracts

Newman Electrical Services was founded in 2019 to fix a simple problem: it was hard to find an electrician who would quote fairly, show up when promised, and stand behind their work afterward.

What began with residential repairs and appliance servicing has expanded into solar installation, CCTV and security systems, generator maintenance and smart-home automation — while keeping the same team accountable for every job on the books.

Today the company holds a full electrical contracting license and carries public liability insurance on every project, residential or commercial.
